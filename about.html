I studied graphic design and worked full time
as a graphic designer in the advertising sector.

I developed an interest in other creative areas
and took illustration and animation classes
where i started to work on my own projects
and experiment with various techniques such
as collage and photo manipulation.

My objective as a graphic designer is to deliver
a complete piece of work both in terms of
aesthetics and functionality. My development
as a professional as well as a creative person
focuses on the enhancement of my personal
style by experimenting with new techniques
and trends.
